GOVERNMENT OF ANDHRA PRADESH
INFRASTRUCTURE & INVESTMENT DEPARTMENT
REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)

Tender ID: AP-ROAD-TEST/2025/001
Project Title: Construction of Four-Lane Highway
Estimated Budget: Rs. 5,00,00,000 (Five Crore Rupees)
Bid Type: Open Tender

1. SCOPE OF WORK
- Construction of 10 km rural road with BT surface
- IRC specifications compliance
- Drainage and signage systems
- Bridge construction at 2 locations
- Quality assurance and testing

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- ISO 9001:2015 certification mandatory
- Minimum 10 years experience in road construction
- Annual turnover: Rs. 10 crores minimum (last 3 years)
- Valid contractor license (Class-1 or equivalent)
- Completed at least 3 similar projects in last 5 years

3. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Quality standards: IRC specifications
- Technical team: Minimum 5 qualified civil engineers
- Own machinery and equipment requirements
- Safety compliance standards (ISO 45001)
- Environmental clearance required

4. FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS
- EMD (Earnest Money Deposit): Rs. 10,00,000 (2% of estimated value)
- Performance guarantee: 10% of contract value
- Payment terms: 30-60-90 days milestone-based
- Bank guarantee from scheduled bank required
- Defect liability period: 24 months

5. SUBMISSION GUIDELINES
- Technical Bid Submission: 31-Jan-2025, 5:00 PM IST
- Financial Bid Opening: 02-Feb-2025, 11:00 AM IST
- Online submission via GeM portal
- Document formats accepted: PDF, DOC, DOCX
- Digital signatures required

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- Technical Capability: 70% weightage
  * Past experience: 20 points
  * Technical team: 15 points
  * Equipment & machinery: 15 points
  * Past performance: 15 points
  * Safety record: 5 points
- Financial Bid: 30% weightage
  * L1 bidder gets full 30 points
  * Others scored proportionally
- Past Performance Bonus: Up to 5 points
  * Awards/recognitions: 2 points
  * On-time completion record: 2 points
  * Client satisfaction ratings: 1 point

7. CONTRACT TERMS AND CONDITIONS
- Contract duration: 12 months from date of commencement
- Extension clause: Up to 3 months with prior approval
- Penalty for delays: 1% of contract value per month
- Quality assurance: Third-party testing mandatory
- Progress reporting: Monthly submission required
- Site inspection: Weekly by department officials

8. PERFORMANCE SECURITY
- Bank guarantee from scheduled bank
- Performance bond: 10% of contract value
- Validity: Contract period + 6 months
- Encashment: In case of breach of contract

9. GOVERNMENT GUIDELINES COMPLIANCE
- General Financial Rules (GFR) 2017, Sections 173-204
- AP State Procurement Manual 2023, Chapter 5
- GeM Portal Guidelines 2024, Version 3.2
- IRC Road Construction Standards
- Environmental Protection Act compliance
- Labor Laws compliance (Minimum Wages Act, etc.)
